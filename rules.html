1. Getting Started

Welcome to the Scavenger Hunt! To begin your operation, you'll need to Enlist in the Hunt from the main page.

  • Alias: Choose your personal player name.
  • Team Name: Join an existing team by typing their exact name, or create a new one! Work together with your team to maximize your score.
  • Discord Link (Optional): If you want to use Discord bot commands alongside the web app, enter your numeric Discord User ID. You can also link this later from your Team Panel.

2. Bounties (Challenges)

The core of the game is completing Bounties scattered throughout the map. Each bounty has a point value, a category, and a description of what you need to do.

  • Availability: Some bounties are infinite, while others have a strict claim limit across the entire game (e.g., "1 remaining"). Once a bounty is exhausted, no one else can claim it!
  • Categories: Pay attention to the category (e.g., Photo, Video, Text). If a bounty requires a Photo or Video, you'll be prompted to upload evidence when claiming.
  • Map View: Toggle the map view on the main page to see where location-based bounties are hiding.

3. Claiming Points

When you've completed a bounty's objective, hit the Claim button. If required, upload your photo or video evidence. Once submitted, your team's score will instantly increase!

Mistake? If you claimed something by accident, you can Undo Claim. This will move the bounty into your team's inventory instead of erasing it completely.

4. Your Inventory (Backpack Mechanics)

The Inventory is where strategy comes into play. Instead of claiming a bounty immediately, you can Stow it in your inventory.

  • Growing Value: While a bounty is stowed in your inventory, its point value steadily increases every minute! The longer you hold it before completing and claiming it, the more it's worth.
  • Capacity Limit: Your team can only hold a limited number of bounties at a time (usually 3 slots).
  • Random Drops (indicated by 🎲): You can request a Random Drop from your Team Panel. The system will assign you a random available bounty, and as a reward for the uncertainty, it instantly gets a massive point boost (typically +40%) on top of the base value!
  • Dropping Items: If you Stow a bounty but realize you can't complete it, you can Drop it to free up an inventory slot. Warning: Dropping a bounty permanently blacklists it for your team. You will never be able to claim it again!

5. Winning the Operation

Keep an eye on the Operation Status at the top of the screen. When the game ends, the podium will be revealed! Note: Scores may be hidden on the main leaderboard during the final stretch of the game to build suspense.

Quick Strategy Guide

  • Always be Stowing: Never leave your inventory empty. Stow high-value bounties early so they accumulate bonus points while you work on other tasks.
  • Embrace the Random: If you don't know what to do next, request a Random Drop. The instant point boost makes even low-value bounties worth completing.
  • Divide and Conquer: Coordinate with your team. Since you share the same inventory and score, make sure you aren't all traveling to the same low-point bounty when you could be splitting up to cover more ground.
  • Watch the Limits: Keep an eye on high-value bounties with limited claims. If another team grabs it before you, you lose the opportunity!
